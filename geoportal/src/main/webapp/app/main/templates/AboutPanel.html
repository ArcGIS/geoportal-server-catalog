Geospatial Inventory Browser

This application allows you to find both current and archived TVA geospatial resources.

How to Use This Application

Narrowing Results

This application works very much like a retail site where you try to find a specific product meeting certain specifications. On the left hand side, you see a panel with a number of options that allow you to filter the content of the Inventory via simple point-and-click actions. The individual options ('Map', 'Time Period', 'Owner', etc) are called 'facets'.

For example, if you are interested in items on 'Linen', you would filter based on the Type Media facet to open its contents. You will see a list of type media and an inidication of how many items in the Inventory are of the different types. Select your favorite type and the Inventory will filter contents to only show those items that have your selected media type.

You may combine multiple facets when finding the specific content. Once you select a facet, the other facets will be updated to reflect the remaining set of items in the Inventory. Try selecting the Media Type 'Linen' and then open the facet 'Disposition'. You will notice that the different dispositions correspond to those items on linen. This mechanism is very similar to the filter capability in Microsoft Excel.

Broadening Results

If you select a facet, you will see it listed in the 'Filters' section above the result. If you click on the X in one of the listed filters, this will clear the choice for the corresponding facet from the filter and you will notice that additional results are displayed and also that the facets now represent the broader resultset. The mechanism of narrowing and broadening results gives you a lot of flexibility in finding the content that fits your need.

Using Results

Once you have located your desired result(s), there are a couple things you can do. Every result item has a link labeled 'Details'. Click this to see the full information known about the Inventory item.

For archive items included in the Inventory the details are limited to the know information such as disposition, media type, data category, etc. For map services, you may see a link to the map service
